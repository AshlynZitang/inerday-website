Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Account information (name, email) via Google or Apple Sign-In
  • Entries, gratitude notes, and other content you create
  • Calendar events you choose to sync
  • Pet interaction data (treats, food counts, preferences)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Inerday service
  • Generate AI-powered daily reports and pet interactions
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Send you notifications (with your permission)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure you can only access your own data. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL.

4. AI Processing

When generating daily reports and pet conversations, your entry content is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic/Claude) for processing. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored by the AI provider beyond the request/response cycle. Momo may remember personal facts you share in conversation to provide a more personalized experience.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In for authentication
  • Google Calendar API for event syncing
  • Supabase for data storage
  • Anthropic (Claude) for AI features

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with:

  • Service providers necessary to operate the App
  • Law enforcement when required by law

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time through the Privacy settings in the App.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Export your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of non-essential data processing

9. Children's Privacy

Inerday is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@inerday.com.
